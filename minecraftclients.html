Minecraft Clients

Minecraft Clients are a way of playing minecraft with official Mojang or Microsoft accounts, but they also include their own set of mods, One of the key mods in most clients are FPS boosters, and FPS display, another key feature would be the zoom function (Powered by Optifine), they also have sets of mods built for specific things on a certain server, such as the skyblock mods for Hypixel.


Here are some Minecraft Clients I feel are good all over:

  • Lunar Client
  • Badlion Client
  • Technic Launcher
